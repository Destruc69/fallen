Archival of ForgeWurst-Fallen

Summary

It was time for me to update Fallen to a newer version from the existing version. Initially, I attempted to create a new project with version 1.16.5 and transfer ForgeWurst-Fallen to the new project, debugging everything due to API changes. However, the stress became overwhelming, and it became evident that starting fresh was necessary. Consequently, I opted to acquire a base, clear it out, and integrate modules from ForgeWurst-Fallen into the new project with a new clickgui and everything else.

Simply, navigate to the Fallen folder in your Minecraft directory and delete the modules directory and provide the crash report to me via (preferably) Discord, or GitHub

How do i use Fallens 'Tones' module and the built in .wav file format player?

Firstly, gather a collection of your favorite songs on YouTube, copy the share link for each of the songs, and go to 'loader.to'. Enter your links and choose the file format 'WAV'. Download all your songs from the links, which are now your songs in WAV format, and navigate to the 'Fallen' folder in the Minecraft directory. Open the 'music' folder and drag and drop the files you just downloaded.

Now, once you have transferred the files, open up Fallen and enter a world. Open the ClickGUI and, under the 'client' category, right-click on the "Tones" module and configure it to your liking. Lastly, enable the module, and a HUD will display the current song playing, and you should hear the song.

Loader.to does have popup ads and sometimes scams. Simply close any window opened that is not related to Loader.to. But it does work and is the one I use.
