Welcome to Fallen

Embark on a journey with Fallen, a dynamic Minecraft anarchy client born from the essence of ForgeWurst. Explore our powerful features and elevate your anarchy experience to new heights.

Established: 2021-10-05

Discover the Features

Embrace Anarchy

Discover the thrill of chaos in Anarchy gameplay with Fallen. Experience a world of enhancements that redefine the boundaries of freedom.

Built on ForgeWurst

Fallen is firmly rooted in the foundation of ForgeWurst, bringing to life an extensive client designed for unparalleled exploration in the world of anarchy.

Your Unique Interface

Craft your user interface as a testament to your style. Choose from a variety of themes, layouts, and colors to let your personality shine through.

Unleash Your Exploits

Tap into the immense power of Fallen with a range of exploits and utilities. From fastbreak and X-ray to Freecam, you have the control to shape the game as you see fit.

Dominate in Combat

Rise victorious in PvP battles with the assistance of tools like Kill Aura, Auto Totem, and Reach. Assert your dominance on the battlefield.

Shape the World

Take command of reality with abilities like Scaffold Walk, NoFall, SafeWalk, and Auto Eat. Traverse the terrain effortlessly and carve your destiny.
