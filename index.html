Welcome to Fallen

Embark on a journey with Fallen, a dynamic Minecraft anarchy client born from the essence of ForgeWurst. Explore our powerful features and elevate your anarchy experience to new heights.

Established: 2021-10-05

Discover the Features

Anarchy at Heart

Fallen thrives in the chaos of anarchy gameplay, offering an array of enhancements that redefine the boundaries of freedom.

Built on ForgeWurst

Fallen stands on the shoulders of ForgeWurst, breathing life into an extensive client designed for unparalleled anarchy exploration.

Forge Your Interface

Craft your user interface as a testament to your style. Choose themes, layouts, and colors, and let your personality shine through.

Masterful Exploits

Unleash the power of Fallen with exploits and utilities, from fastbreak and X-ray to Freecam. Control the game as you see fit.

Dominate in Combat

Rise victorious in PvP battles with tools like Kill Aura, Auto Totem, and Reach. Claim your supremacy on the battlefield.

Shape the World

Manipulate reality with Scaffold Walk, NoFall, SafeWalk, and Auto Eat. Traverse the terrain effortlessly and shape your destiny.
